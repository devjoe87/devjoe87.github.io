Raven Hall Lodges

Lodges

If you are searching for a unique and alternative option for Robin Hood’s Bay accommodation, why not take a look at our exclusive holiday lodges?

With fantastic views overlooking Robin Hoods Bay and across the North Yorkshire coast, our eight exclusive environmentally-friendly lodges have been designed in Finland and hand-built with the finest timber.

Our lodges are only a 20-minute drive from Scarborough, making them an ideal base if you are searching for holiday lodge accommodation in the area. One of the four Bay Lodges is also dog friendly, so you can bring your best four-legged friend on holiday to Raven Hall too!

Lodge Types and Tariff

Raven Hall is a versatile hotel, ideal for a quiet retreat in beautiful surroundings offering a great base between Whitby and Scarborough, it also makes a perfect venue for weddings and conferences.

BAY STUDIO

The Bay Studio Lodges all feature log burners and a patio with table and chairs looking over Robin Hood’s Bay and across to the North Yorkshire Moors.

Prices from £120.00

MOORS LODGE

The Moors Lodges feature a comfortable living area, patio and a small balcony upstairs looking over Robin Hood’s Bay across to the North Yorkshire Moors.

Prices from £155.00

PANORAMA

The Panorama Lodges are located on the first floor with an open plan living and dining area, and two balconies looking across Robin Hood’s Bay to the North Yorkshire Moors.

Prices from £155.00

Our eight holiday lodges include a unique range of features for guests, including comfortable furnishings and fittings, log burning stoves, their own kitchenettes (for light snacks) and wireless internet access. All lodges have stunning Bay views with either patio or balconies.

In addition to the sustainable material used in construction, operational aspects of our Scarborough log cabins include water-recycling systems, recycled energy boosting and log burning stoves. All lodges have Wi-Fi, DVD players on request and a kitchen area allowing guests to enjoy breakfast and light meals. Alternatively, guests are welcome to visit the Panorama Restaurant at Raven Hall Hotel for delicious meals throughout the day, or our Bay View Lounge for a less formal evening meal.

Our holiday lodges benefit from full housekeeping services daily, as in the main Raven Hall Hotel. Guests staying in our log cabin accommodation will have full use of all Hotel facilities, including the Panorama Restaurant.

For more information about our holiday lodges near Scarborough, please call 01723 870353.
