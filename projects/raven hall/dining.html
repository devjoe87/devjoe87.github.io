Panorama Restaurant

Dining

The Panorama Restaurant at Raven Hall offers both stunning Bay Views with delicious locally sourced, fresh food prepared by our team of Chefs. Start the day with a full English Breakfast and look out over the magnificent landscape before heading off to explore Robin Hood's Bay and in the evenings, enjoy the sunset and view of the Bay with a candlelit dinner.


DINNER

The evening Tables D'Hotes menu changes daily with choices to suit all palates including steaks and an extensive vegetarian menu, special dietary option on request and a children's menu for the younger ones. The ideal venue for a special Dinner with breathtaking views and sunsets.

For those wishing to have a less formal meal during the day or evening, The Bay View Lounge menu has some lighter options and guests can relax in this small, light lounge area which overlooks our gardens and that same wonderful Bay View.

VIEW THE EVENING MENU


LUNCH AND AFTERNOON TEA

During the day an extensive lunch menu is available in the Panorama Restaurant or sandwiches, snacks, coffees and cakes in the main lounge, Bay View Lounge and Bar or in the beautiful landscaped gardens for those warmer days. Traditional Afternoon Tea is served from 2.30pm daily and tea and cakes all day.

SUNDAY LUNCH CARVERY

The famous Raven Hall Sunday Carvery is served in the Panorama Restaurant all year round and is the perfect dining experience for the whole family. A walk in the grounds to enjoy the view further is a must after lunch.
