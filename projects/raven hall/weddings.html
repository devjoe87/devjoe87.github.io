Your Big Day

Weddings

Raven Hall Hotel in Ravenscar, between Scarborough and Whitby, has become one of the most popular venues on the North Yorkshire Coast and has one of the finest settings for your wedding, civil ceremony, reception and wedding breakfast. The idyllic location of the Hotel, with its cliff-top views of Robin Hood’s Bay out to sea and over the North Yorkshire Moors, provides the dream location for your dream wedding.


Raven Hall Hotel has a choice of Banqueting Suites for your wedding depending on how many guests will be attending. We recommend that you make an appointment to view the wedding venue, when our experienced wedding team can show you around, discuss your options and costs for your special day.

The hotel itself has a civil licence, which enables both the wedding ceremony and reception to take place in one location. We have a choice of rooms, from the original Great Hall to the Viking Suite dependent on the number of people attending your wedding ceremony. We also have the Good Hope Chapel for an additional service of blessing if you wish.


Now offering Outdoor Weddings, with the perfect location for your Ceremony, with outstanding views across Robin Hoods Bay, making this one of the most unique venues on the Yorkshire Coast. This is a romantic setting that you and your guests will never forget. (Outdoor wedding ceremonies are weather permitting and an alternative indoor ceremony room is always provided.)


Accommodation at Raven Hall, including our Lodges, is available for your wedding guests and a complimentary room is also offered to the Happy Couple. We are able to cater for up to 120 guests in the Great Hall, creating a superb atmosphere for both the civil ceremony and evening wedding reception. We are able to cater for up to 100 guests in the George III Suite for the Wedding Breakfast.
