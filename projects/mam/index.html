About

About Me

Hi! I'm Matthew.

A Production Music Composer and Composer/Mixing Engineer based in Norwich, Norfolk.

I specialise in music for Video Games, TV and Film.

In the last year I have been involved in notable projects for voice direction in Divinity: Original Sin 2 aswell as working with Side Audio UK on upcoming AAA games.

Previously, I worked for 5 years as a composer and mix engineer for Pitstop Productions in an all purpose high end studio facility.

During my time there I was part of the TIGA award winning audio team - Winning the "Best Audio Services 2017 Award" and the "Best Educational Game 2016 Award" with Times Table Adventure.

Whilst at Pitstop Productions I got to work with Major companies such as:

  • Sony: Invizimals, PS Pets
  • SEGA: Crazy Taxi
  • Ubisoft: Assassins Creed Series
  • Rovio: Angry Birds Action (Composer, Mixing Engineer)
  • Codemasters: Grid Autosport, F1 series
  • Firesprite: The Persistence VR (PS4) (Composer, Mixing Engineer)
  • As well as many other smaller companies on upcoming games for various platforms
